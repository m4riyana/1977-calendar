Maundy Thursday, known as Huwebes Santo in the Philippines, is part of the Holy Week observances leading up to Easter. It commemorates the Last Supper of Jesus Christ with his apostles, an important event in Christian faith symbolizing humility, service, and sacrifice.

In the Philippines, churches hold special masses and processions. Many Filipinos also take part in the tradition of visiting seven churches, known as Visita Iglesia.
